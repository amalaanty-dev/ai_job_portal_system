31. Healthcare Statistician / Biostatistician
Job Summary
Applies statistical methods to healthcare data for research, clinical trials, and policy-making.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and analyze clinical trials
• Apply statistical techniques to healthcare datasets
• Interpret and report statistical findings
• Ensure data validity and reliability
• Collaborate with researchers and public health experts
Requirements
• Master’s/PhD in Statistics, Biostatistics, or related field
• Experience in healthcare or clinical research
Skills
• Statistical tools (SAS, R, SPSS)
• Research methodology
• Data interpretation
