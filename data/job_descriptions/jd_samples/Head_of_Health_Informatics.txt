25. Head of Health Informatics
Job Summary
Leads the integration of healthcare data, technology, and clinical systems to improve patient
care and operational efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Oversee health informatics systems (EHR/EMR)
• Lead digital transformation initiatives
• Ensure interoperability of healthcare systems
• Collaborate with clinicians and IT teams
• Drive innovation in healthcare technology
Requirements
• Degree in Health Informatics, IT, or Healthcare Management
• 8–12 years of experience
Skills
• Health informatics expertise
• System integration
• Leadership & innovation
