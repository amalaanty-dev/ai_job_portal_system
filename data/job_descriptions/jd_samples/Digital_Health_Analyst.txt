54. Digital Health Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes data from digital health platforms (mobile apps, remote monitoring systems, health
tech tools) to improve healthcare delivery and patient engagement.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze user and patient data from digital health platforms
• Monitor app performance, engagement, and outcomes
• Generate insights to improve digital health solutions
• Collaborate with product and tech teams
• Support digital transformation initiatives
Requirements
• Degree in Health Informatics, Data Analytics, or IT
• Experience with digital health tools or platforms
Skills
• Data analytics & visualization
• Product analytics
• Understanding of digital health ecosystems
