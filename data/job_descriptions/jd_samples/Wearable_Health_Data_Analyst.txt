60. Wearable Health Data Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes data from wearable devices (fitness trackers, smartwatches, medical wearables) to
monitor health and wellness trends.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and analyze wearable device data (heart rate, activity, sleep)
• Identify health patterns and anomalies
• Support preventive healthcare initiatives
• Collaborate with digital health and research teams
• Develop reports and dashboards
Requirements
• Degree in Data Science, Health Informatics, or related field
• Experience with IoT or wearable data preferred
Skills
• Data analytics
• IoT data understanding
• Visualization tools
