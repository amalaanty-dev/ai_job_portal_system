3. Healthcare Reporting Analyst
Job Summary
Focuses on generating reports and dashboards for hospitals, insurance companies, or
healthcare organizations.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop routine and ad-hoc reports
• Create dashboards using Power BI/Tableau
• Analyze KPIs like patient flow, revenue, and outcomes
• Automate reporting processes
• Communicate insights to management
Requirements
• Degree in Data Analytics, IT, or Healthcare Management
• Experience with reporting tools
Skills
• Data visualization
• Report automation
• Communication skills
