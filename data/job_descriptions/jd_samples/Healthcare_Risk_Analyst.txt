58. Healthcare Risk Analyst
Job Summary
Identifies and analyzes risks in healthcare systems related to operations, compliance, and
patient safety.
Key Responsibilities
• Assess operational and clinical risks
• Analyze incident and safety data
• Develop risk mitigation strategies
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations
• Prepare risk assessment reports
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare, Risk Management, or Analytics
• Experience in healthcare risk or compliance
Skills
• Risk analysis
• Data interpretation
• Regulatory knowledge
