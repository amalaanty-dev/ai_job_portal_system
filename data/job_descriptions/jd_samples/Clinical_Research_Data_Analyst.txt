32. Clinical Research Data Analyst
Job Summary
Supports clinical research by collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data from clinical studies
and trials.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect, clean, and validate clinical research data
• Analyze datasets from clinical trials and observational studies
• Generate reports for research teams and sponsors
• Ensure compliance with regulatory standards (GCP, FDA)
• Collaborate with researchers, clinicians, and statisticians
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Pharmacy, or Data Analytics
• 2–5 years of experience in clinical research or analytics
Skills
• Clinical data management systems (CDMS)
• SQL, Excel, SAS/R
• Attention to detail and regulatory compliance
