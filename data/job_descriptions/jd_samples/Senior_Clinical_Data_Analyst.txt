10. Senior Clinical Data Analyst
Job Summary
Leads clinical data analysis efforts to support research, treatment evaluation, and clinical
trials.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze complex clinical datasets and trial data
• Design data models for clinical research
• Ensure compliance with clinical standards (FDA, GCP)
• Mentor junior analysts
• Collaborate with physicians and research teams
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Pharmacy, or Data Analytics
• 3–5 years of clinical data experience
Skills
• Clinical research knowledge
• Statistical analysis (SAS/R)
• Data validation & compliance
