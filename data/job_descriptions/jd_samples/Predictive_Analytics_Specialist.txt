30. Predictive Analytics Specialist
(Healthcare)
Job Summary
Focuses on forecasting healthcare trends and outcomes using advanced statistical and
machine learning techniques.
Key Responsibilities
• Build predictive models (patient risk, demand forecasting)
• Analyze historical data to forecast trends
• Support preventive healthcare strategies
• Develop predictive dashboards
• Collaborate with stakeholders for implementation
Requirements
• Degree in Statistics, Data Science, or related field
• Experience in predictive analytics
Skills
• Predictive modeling
• Statistical analysis
• Data visualization
