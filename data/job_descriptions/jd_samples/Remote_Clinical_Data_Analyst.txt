66. Remote Clinical Data Analyst
Job Summary
Works remotely to analyze clinical and healthcare data, supporting hospitals, research
organizations, or CROs.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze clinical trial and patient data
• Prepare reports and dashboards remotely
• Ensure data quality and compliance
• Collaborate with global teams virtually
• Support research and clinical operations
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Healthcare, or Data Analytics
• Experience in clinical data analysis
Skills
• Clinical data knowledge
• Remote collaboration tools
• Data analysis & reporting
