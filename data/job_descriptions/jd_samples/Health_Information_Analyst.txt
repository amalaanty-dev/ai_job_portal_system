5. Health Information Analyst
Job Summary
Manages and analyzes health information systems and patient data to ensure quality and
compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Maintain and manage patient health records
• Ensure data integrity and security
• Analyze health information for reporting
• Support healthcare IT systems
• Ensure compliance with healthcare laws (HIPAA, etc.)
Requirements
• Degree in Health Information Management or related field
• Knowledge of EHR/EMR systems
Skills
• Data governance
• Record management
• Attention to detail
