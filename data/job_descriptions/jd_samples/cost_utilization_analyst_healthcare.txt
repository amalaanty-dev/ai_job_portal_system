48. Cost & Utilization Analyst (Healthcare)
Job Summary
Analyzes healthcare resource utilization and cost patterns to improve efficiency and reduce
unnecessary expenses.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze patient utilization data (hospital stays, procedures)
• Identify cost drivers and inefficiencies
• Support utilization management programs
• Develop cost optimization strategies
• Prepare reports for decision-makers
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare, Economics, or Data Analytics
• Experience in healthcare analytics or utilization review
Skills
• Cost analysis
• Data modeling
• Healthcare operations knowledge
