34. Biostatistician
Job Summary
Applies statistical methods to analyze clinical trial data and support medical research.
Key Responsibilities
• Design statistical analysis plans (SAP)
• Analyze clinical trial and healthcare datasets
• Interpret statistical results and generate reports
• Collaborate with researchers on study design
• Ensure statistical validity and compliance
Requirements
• Master’s/PhD in Biostatistics or Statistics
• Experience in clinical research preferred
Skills
• SAS, R, SPSS
• Statistical modeling
• Research methodology
