39. Clinical Informatics Analyst
Job Summary
Focuses on improving clinical workflows and patient care by analyzing and optimizing
clinical information systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze clinical workflows and system usage
• Optimize clinical systems (EHR, lab systems, etc.)
• Support clinicians with data-driven insights
• Develop clinical reports and dashboards
• Train healthcare staff on system usage
Requirements
• Degree in Health Informatics, Nursing, or related field
• Experience in clinical environments
Skills
• Clinical workflow analysis
• Data visualization
• Communication & training
