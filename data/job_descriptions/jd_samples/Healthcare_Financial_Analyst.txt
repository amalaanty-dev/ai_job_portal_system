44. Healthcare Financial Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes financial data within healthcare organizations to support budgeting, forecasting, and
cost management.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze financial statements, budgets, and forecasts
• Monitor healthcare costs, revenue, and profitability
• Identify cost-saving opportunities
• Prepare financial reports for management
• Support strategic financial planning
Requirements
• Degree in Finance, Accounting, Healthcare Management, or Analytics
• 2–5 years of experience in financial analysis
Skills
• Financial modeling
• Excel, SQL
• Analytical thinking
