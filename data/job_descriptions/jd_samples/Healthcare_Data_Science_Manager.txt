22. Healthcare Data Science Manager
Job Summary
Leads data science teams focused on advanced analytics, machine learning, and AI in
healthcare.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage data scientists and ML engineers
• Develop predictive and AI-driven healthcare models
• Oversee deployment of analytics solutions
• Ensure ethical use of healthcare data
• Align AI initiatives with organizational strategy
Requirements
• Master’s/PhD in Data Science, AI, or related field
• 6–10 years of experience in analytics/data science
Skills
• Machine learning & AI
• Team leadership
• Advanced analytics
