9. Healthcare Data Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes complex healthcare datasets to support clinical, operational, and financial decision-
making.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect, clean, and analyze large healthcare datasets
• Develop dashboards and reports (Power BI/Tableau)
• Identify trends in patient care, costs, and outcomes
• Collaborate with clinical and administrative teams
• Ensure data accuracy and regulatory compliance
Requirements
• Bachelor’s degree in Healthcare, Data Analytics, or related field
• 2–4 years of experience in healthcare analytics
Skills
• SQL, Python/R
• Data visualization
• Healthcare domain knowledge
