43. Health Information Systems Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes and supports healthcare information systems to ensure efficient operations and data
management.
Key Responsibilities
• Evaluate healthcare IT systems and workflows
• Troubleshoot system issues and improve performance
• Analyze system data for reporting and optimization
• Support system upgrades and maintenance
• Ensure compliance with healthcare standards
Requirements
• Degree in Health Information Management, IT, or related field
• Experience in healthcare IT systems
Skills
• System analysis
• Data reporting
• Technical troubleshooting
