18. Claims Data Analyst (Insurance)
Job Summary
Analyzes insurance claims data to detect trends, reduce fraud, and improve claims
processing.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze healthcare insurance claims data
• Identify fraudulent or suspicious claims
• Monitor claims processing efficiency
• Generate reports for insurance teams
• Ensure compliance with insurance policies
Requirements
• Degree in Finance, Insurance, or Healthcare Analytics
• Experience in claims processing or insurance analytics
Skills
• Claims analysis
• Fraud detection
• Data interpretation
