50. Health Policy Analyst
Job Summary
Evaluates healthcare policies and provides data-driven recommendations to improve
healthcare systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze healthcare policies and their impact
• Conduct research on healthcare systems and reforms
• Use data to support policy recommendations
• Prepare policy briefs and reports
• Collaborate with government bodies and NGOs
Requirements
• Degree in Public Health, Public Policy, or Healthcare Management
• Experience in policy research or analysis
Skills
• Policy analysis
• Research and writing
• Critical thinking
