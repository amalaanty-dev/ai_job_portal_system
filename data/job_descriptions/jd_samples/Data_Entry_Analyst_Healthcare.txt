6. Data Entry Analyst (Healthcare)
Job Summary
Responsible for entering, updating, and maintaining healthcare data in systems with high
accuracy.
Key Responsibilities
• Input patient and medical data into systems
• Verify and correct data errors
• Maintain data confidentiality
• Organize and manage records
• Assist in basic data reporting
Requirements
• Any graduate (Healthcare background preferred)
• Basic computer and typing skills
Skills
• Fast and accurate typing
• Attention to detail
• Data validation
