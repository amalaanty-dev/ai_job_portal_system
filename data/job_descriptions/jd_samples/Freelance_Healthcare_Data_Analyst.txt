62. Freelance Healthcare Data Analyst
Job Summary
Works independently on short-term or project-based healthcare data analysis assignments for
hospitals, startups, or consulting firms.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect, clean, and analyze healthcare datasets
• Create reports, dashboards, and insights for clients
• Work on diverse projects (clinical, financial, operational data)
• Communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders
• Manage multiple client projects and deadlines
Requirements
• Degree in Data Analytics, Healthcare, or related field
• Proven portfolio of analytics projects
Skills
• SQL, Excel, Python/R
• Data visualization (Power BI/Tableau)
• Client communication & time management
