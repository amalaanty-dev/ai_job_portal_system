17. Healthcare BI (Business Intelligence)
Analyst
Job Summary
Designs and manages BI solutions to provide actionable insights in healthcare organizations.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and maintain BI dashboards and reports
• Design data models and data warehouses
• Integrate data from multiple healthcare systems
• Support data-driven decision-making
• Ensure data quality and consistency
Requirements
• Degree in IT, Data Science, or related field
• Experience with BI tools (Power BI, Tableau, SQL)
Skills
• Data modeling
• BI tools expertise
• SQL & ETL processes
