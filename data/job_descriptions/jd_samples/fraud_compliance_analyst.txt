59. Fraud & Compliance Analyst
(Healthcare)
Job Summary
Detects fraudulent activities and ensures compliance with healthcare regulations and
standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze claims and billing data for fraud detection
• Identify suspicious patterns and anomalies
• Conduct compliance audits
• Prepare investigation reports
• Work with legal and compliance teams
Requirements
• Degree in Finance, Healthcare, or Analytics
• Experience in fraud detection or compliance
Skills
• Fraud analytics
• Data mining
• Attention to detail
