4. Medical Data Analyst
Job Summary
Works with medical datasets to support healthcare providers in improving diagnosis,
treatment, and operational efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze medical records and treatment data
• Identify patterns in diseases and treatments
• Support clinical decision-making
• Ensure data privacy and accuracy
• Collaborate with healthcare professionals
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare, Biology, or Data Analytics
• Basic understanding of medical terminology
Skills
• Data analysis
• Healthcare domain knowledge
• Problem-solving
