24. Chief Data Officer (Healthcare
Organization)
Job Summary
Executive-level role responsible for enterprise-wide data strategy, governance, and
innovation.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and implement enterprise data strategy
• Establish data governance and compliance frameworks
• Drive data-driven culture across the organization
• Oversee data security, privacy, and quality
• Collaborate with C-suite executives
Requirements
• Advanced degree (MBA, PhD, or equivalent)
• 12+ years of experience in data leadership
Skills
• Executive leadership
• Data governance & compliance
• Strategic vision
