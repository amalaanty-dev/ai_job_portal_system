14. Healthcare Operations Analyst
Job Summary
Improves hospital or healthcare facility operations using data analytics.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze operational data (patient flow, staffing, resources)
• Optimize scheduling and resource allocation
• Identify inefficiencies and recommend solutions
• Support operational decision-making
• Collaborate with hospital management
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare Management, Operations, or Analytics
• 2–5 years of relevant experience
Skills
• Operations analysis
• Data visualization
• Problem-solving
