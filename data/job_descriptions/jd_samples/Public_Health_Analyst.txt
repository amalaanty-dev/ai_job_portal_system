49. Public Health Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes population health data to improve community health outcomes and support public
health initiatives.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and analyze public health datasets (disease trends, demographics)
• Monitor health indicators and identify risk factors
• Support public health programs and interventions
• Prepare reports for government and health agencies
• Collaborate with epidemiologists and policymakers
Requirements
• Degree in Public Health, Epidemiology, or Statistics
• 2–5 years of experience in health data analysis
Skills
• Statistical analysis (R, SPSS, Python)
• Data visualization
• Public health knowledge
