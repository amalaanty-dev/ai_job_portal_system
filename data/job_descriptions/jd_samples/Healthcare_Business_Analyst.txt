11. Healthcare Business Analyst
Job Summary
Bridges the gap between healthcare operations and IT/data teams to improve systems and
processes.
Key Responsibilities
• Gather and document business requirements
• Analyze workflows and recommend improvements
• Work with IT teams on healthcare system implementations
• Develop reports and dashboards
• Support decision-making with data insights
Requirements
• Degree in Business, Healthcare Management, or IT
• Experience in healthcare systems (EHR/EMR)
Skills
• Business analysis
• Stakeholder communication
• Process improvement
