15. Revenue Cycle Data Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes financial and billing data to optimize the healthcare revenue cycle.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze billing, coding, and claims data
• Identify revenue leakage and inefficiencies
• Monitor key revenue cycle metrics
• Support process improvements in billing and collections
• Work with finance and coding teams
Requirements
• Degree in Finance, Healthcare, or Analytics
• Experience in medical billing/coding
Skills
• Revenue cycle management
• Data analysis
• Financial reporting
