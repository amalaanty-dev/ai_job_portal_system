53. Global Health Data Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes international health data to address global health challenges and support
multinational health initiatives.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze global health datasets (WHO, World Bank, etc.)
• Monitor international health trends and disease patterns
• Support global health programs and research
• Collaborate with international organizations
• Prepare reports and insights for global stakeholders
Requirements
• Degree in Global Health, Public Health, or Data Analytics
• Experience in international health data analysis
Skills
• Cross-country data analysis
• Research and reporting
• Knowledge of global health systems
