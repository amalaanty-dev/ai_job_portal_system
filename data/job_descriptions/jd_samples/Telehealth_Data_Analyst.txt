55. Telehealth Data Analyst
Job Summary
Focuses on analyzing telemedicine and virtual care data to enhance remote healthcare
services.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze telehealth usage, patient interactions, and outcomes
• Monitor virtual consultation performance metrics
• Identify trends in remote care delivery
• Optimize telehealth workflows and systems
• Provide insights for service improvement
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare, IT, or Analytics
• Experience with telehealth platforms preferred
Skills
• Data analysis
• Remote healthcare knowledge
• Dashboard creation
