20. Lead Data Analyst (Healthcare)
Job Summary
Oversees a team of analysts and ensures delivery of high-quality data solutions aligned with
organizational goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and manage a team of data analysts
• Define analytics strategies and roadmaps
• Ensure data quality and governance standards
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams
• Drive data-driven decision-making across departments
Requirements
• Degree in Data Analytics, IT, or Healthcare
• 5–8 years of experience with team leadership exposure
Skills
• Team leadership
• Data governance
• Strategic thinking
