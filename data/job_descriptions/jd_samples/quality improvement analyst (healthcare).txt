13. Quality Improvement Analyst
(Healthcare)
Job Summary
Focuses on improving healthcare quality, safety, and compliance through data-driven
insights.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor healthcare quality metrics (KPIs)
• Analyze patient outcomes and safety data
• Identify areas for improvement
• Support accreditation and compliance processes
• Prepare quality reports for leadership
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare Administration or Analytics
• Experience in quality improvement processes
Skills
• KPI tracking
• Process improvement (Lean/Six Sigma)
• Data analysis
