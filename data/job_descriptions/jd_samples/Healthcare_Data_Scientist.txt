26. Healthcare Data Scientist
Job Summary
Applies data science techniques to healthcare data to generate insights, build predictive
models, and improve patient outcomes and operational efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop predictive and prescriptive models (disease risk, readmission, etc.)
• Analyze structured and unstructured healthcare data (EHR, imaging, claims)
• Build and validate machine learning models
• Communicate insights to clinical and business stakeholders
• Collaborate with IT, clinicians, and analysts
Requirements
• Master’s/PhD in Data Science, Computer Science, or related field
• 3–6 years of experience in data science
Skills
• Python, R, SQL
• Machine learning algorithms
• Data visualization & storytelling
