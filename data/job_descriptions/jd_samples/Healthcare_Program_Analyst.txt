52. Healthcare Program Analyst
(Government)
Job Summary
Evaluates and manages healthcare programs run by government agencies to ensure
effectiveness and efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze program data and performance metrics
• Monitor healthcare program outcomes
• Recommend improvements for public health programs
• Prepare reports for government stakeholders
• Ensure compliance with regulations and policies
Requirements
• Degree in Public Health, Healthcare Administration, or Analytics
• Experience in government or public health programs
Skills
• Program evaluation
• Data analysis
• Stakeholder management
