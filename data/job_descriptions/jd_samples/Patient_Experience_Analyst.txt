57. Patient Experience Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes patient feedback and experience data to improve satisfaction and healthcare service
quality.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and analyze patient feedback (surveys, reviews)
• Measure patient satisfaction and experience metrics
• Identify service gaps and improvement areas
• Develop reports and dashboards
• Work with healthcare teams to enhance patient experience
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare Management, Analytics, or related field
• Experience in customer/patient experience analysis
Skills
• Data visualization
• Communication & empathy
• Survey analysis
