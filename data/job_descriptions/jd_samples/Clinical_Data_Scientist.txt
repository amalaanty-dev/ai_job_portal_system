27. Clinical Data Scientist
Job Summary
Specializes in applying data science to clinical trials, patient data, and medical research to
support evidence-based healthcare.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze clinical trial and patient data
• Develop statistical and predictive models for treatment outcomes
• Ensure compliance with clinical standards (GCP, FDA guidelines)
• Work with researchers and healthcare professionals
• Interpret complex clinical datasets
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Biostatistics, or Data Science
• Experience in clinical research or healthcare analytics
Skills
• Clinical data analysis
• Statistical modeling (SAS/R)
• Domain knowledge in medicine
