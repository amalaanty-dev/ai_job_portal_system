1. Healthcare Data Analyst (Junior)
Job Summary
A Healthcare Data Analyst (Junior) supports data collection, cleaning, analysis, and reporting
to improve healthcare decision-making and patient outcomes.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and clean healthcare datasets (patient records, billing data, etc.)
• Perform basic data analysis using Excel, SQL, or Python
• Create reports and dashboards for internal stakeholders
• Assist in identifying trends and patterns in healthcare data
• Ensure data accuracy and compliance with healthcare regulations
Requirements
• Bachelor’s degree in Healthcare, Data Science, Statistics, or related field
• Basic knowledge of Excel, SQL, and data visualization tools
Skills
• Analytical thinking
• Attention to detail
• Basic statistical knowledge
