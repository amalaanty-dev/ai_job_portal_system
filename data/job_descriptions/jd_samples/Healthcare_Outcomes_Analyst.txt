36. Healthcare Outcomes Analyst
Job Summary
Evaluates the effectiveness of healthcare treatments and interventions using data analysis.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze patient outcomes and treatment effectiveness
• Compare clinical interventions and therapies
• Support evidence-based healthcare decisions
• Develop outcome measurement frameworks
• Prepare reports for stakeholders
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare, Public Health, or Analytics
• Experience in outcomes research or analytics
Skills
• Data analysis
• Clinical knowledge
• Reporting and visualization
