16. Healthcare Performance Analyst
Job Summary
Evaluates healthcare performance metrics to improve efficiency and patient outcomes.
Key Responsibilities
• Track performance KPIs across departments
• Analyze productivity and service quality
• Develop performance dashboards
• Provide insights for strategic planning
• Benchmark performance against industry standards
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare Management or Analytics
• Experience in performance analysis
Skills
• KPI analysis
• Data visualization
• Strategic thinking
