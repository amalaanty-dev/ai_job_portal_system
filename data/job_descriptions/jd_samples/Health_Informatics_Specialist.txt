38. Health Informatics Specialist
Job Summary
Bridges healthcare, data, and technology by managing and optimizing health information
systems to improve patient care and operational efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage and optimize Electronic Health Records (EHR/EMR) systems
• Analyze healthcare data to improve workflows and outcomes
• Ensure data accuracy, privacy, and compliance
• Collaborate with clinicians, IT teams, and administrators
• Support digital health initiatives and system upgrades
Requirements
• Degree in Health Informatics, Healthcare IT, or related field
• 2–5 years of experience in healthcare systems
Skills
• EHR/EMR systems knowledge
• Data analysis & reporting
• Healthcare regulations (HIPAA, HL7, etc.)
