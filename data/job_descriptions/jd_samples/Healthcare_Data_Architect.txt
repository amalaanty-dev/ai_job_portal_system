42. Healthcare Data Architect
Job Summary
Designs and manages the overall data architecture for healthcare systems to ensure
scalability, security, and efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Design data models, warehouses, and architecture
• Define data standards and governance policies
• Ensure secure and scalable data infrastructure
• Collaborate with IT and analytics teams
• Optimize data storage and retrieval systems
Requirements
• Degree in Computer Science, IT, or Data Engineering
• 5+ years of experience in data architecture
Skills
• Data modeling
• Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
• Database management
