41. EHR Implementation Analyst
Job Summary
Responsible for implementing, configuring, and supporting Electronic Health Record systems
within healthcare organizations.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in EHR system implementation and configuration
• Gather requirements from clinical and administrative teams
• Test and validate system functionality
• Train end-users on EHR systems
• Provide post-implementation support
Requirements
• Degree in Health Informatics, IT, or Healthcare Management
• Experience with EHR systems (Epic, Cerner, etc.)
Skills
• System implementation
• User training
• Problem-solving
