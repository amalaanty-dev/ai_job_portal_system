23. Director of Healthcare Analytics
Job Summary
Provides strategic leadership for all analytics initiatives within a healthcare organization.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and execute analytics vision and strategy
• Lead multiple analytics teams and departments
• Drive data-driven transformation initiatives
• Collaborate with executive leadership
• Manage budgets and resource allocation
Requirements
• Master’s degree (MBA, Healthcare Management, Data Science)
• 10+ years of experience
Skills
• Strategic leadership
• Business acumen
• Data governance & policy
