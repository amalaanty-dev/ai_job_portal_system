45. Medical Billing Data Analyst
Job Summary
Focuses on analyzing medical billing data to ensure accurate billing, coding, and
reimbursement processes.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze billing and coding data for accuracy
• Identify errors in claims and reimbursement processes
• Monitor billing KPIs and performance
• Support coding teams with data insights
• Ensure compliance with billing regulations
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare, Finance, or related field
• Knowledge of medical coding (ICD, CPT)
Skills
• Billing systems knowledge
• Data analysis
• Attention to detail
