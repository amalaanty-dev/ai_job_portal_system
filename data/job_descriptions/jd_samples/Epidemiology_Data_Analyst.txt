51. Epidemiology Data Analyst
Job Summary
Specializes in analyzing disease patterns and epidemiological data to support public health
decision-making.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze disease surveillance and outbreak data
• Identify trends and risk factors
• Support epidemiological research studies
• Create dashboards and reports for health authorities
• Assist in disease prevention strategies
Requirements
• Degree in Epidemiology, Public Health, or Statistics
• Experience in epidemiological data analysis
Skills
• Statistical modeling
• Data interpretation
• Knowledge of disease surveillance systems
