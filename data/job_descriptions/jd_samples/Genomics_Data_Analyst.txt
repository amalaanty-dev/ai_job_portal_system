61. Genomics Data Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes genetic and genomic data to support personalized medicine, research, and
diagnostics.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze genomic datasets (DNA, RNA sequencing)
• Support research in genetics and precision medicine
• Interpret genetic variations and patterns
• Collaborate with researchers and clinicians
• Develop reports and insights
Requirements
• Degree in Bioinformatics, Genomics, or Life Sciences
• Experience in genomic data analysis
Skills
• Bioinformatics tools (BLAST, Bioconductor)
• Statistical analysis
• Domain knowledge in genetics
