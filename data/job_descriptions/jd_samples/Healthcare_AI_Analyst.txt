56. Healthcare AI Analyst
Job Summary
Supports the development and evaluation of AI-driven healthcare solutions and analytics
models.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze AI model outputs and performance
• Work with data scientists to improve AI algorithms
• Evaluate healthcare AI use cases
• Ensure compliance with ethical AI standards
• Translate AI insights into actionable decisions
Requirements
• Degree in Data Science, AI, or Healthcare Analytics
• Experience with machine learning concepts
Skills
• AI/ML fundamentals
• Data interpretation
• Critical thinking
