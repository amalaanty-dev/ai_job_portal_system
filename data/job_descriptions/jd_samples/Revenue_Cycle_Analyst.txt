47. Revenue Cycle Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes the entire healthcare revenue cycle—from patient registration to final payment—to
improve efficiency and profitability.
Key Responsibilities
• Monitor revenue cycle KPIs (denials, collections, AR days)
• Identify revenue leakage and inefficiencies
• Analyze billing, coding, and claims workflows
• Recommend process improvements
• Generate revenue cycle performance reports
Requirements
• Degree in Healthcare Management, Finance, or Analytics
• Experience in revenue cycle management
Skills
• Revenue cycle knowledge
• Data analysis
• Process improvement
