37. Real-World Evidence (RWE) Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes real-world healthcare data (EHR, claims, registries) to generate insights beyond
clinical trials.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze real-world datasets (EHR, insurance claims, registries)
• Generate evidence on treatment effectiveness and safety
• Support regulatory and market access decisions
• Collaborate with pharma, research, and healthcare teams
• Develop RWE reports and publications
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Epidemiology, or Data Analytics
• Experience in healthcare data analysis
Skills
• Data analysis (SQL, Python, R)
• Understanding of real-world data sources
• Research and reporting
