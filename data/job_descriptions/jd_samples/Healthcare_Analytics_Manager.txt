21. Healthcare Analytics Manager
Job Summary
Manages analytics functions within a healthcare organization, aligning data initiatives with
business and clinical goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Oversee analytics projects and teams
• Develop data strategies for healthcare improvement
• Manage reporting systems and dashboards
• Collaborate with executives and department heads
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations
Requirements
• Master’s degree preferred (Healthcare Management, Data Science)
• 6–10 years of experience
Skills
• Project management
• Healthcare domain expertise
• Stakeholder management
