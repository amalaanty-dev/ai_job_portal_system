35. Epidemiologist (Data-Focused)
Job Summary
Studies disease patterns and health trends using data to improve public health outcomes.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze population health and disease data
• Track outbreaks and health trends
• Conduct epidemiological studies
• Support public health policy decisions
• Prepare reports for health authorities
Requirements
• Degree in Epidemiology or Public Health
• Experience in data analysis and research
Skills
• Statistical analysis
• Public health knowledge
• Data interpretation
