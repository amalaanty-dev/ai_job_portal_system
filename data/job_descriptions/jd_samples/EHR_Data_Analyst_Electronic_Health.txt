8. EHR Data Analyst (Electronic Health
Records)
Job Summary
Focuses on analyzing data from Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems to improve
healthcare delivery.
Key Responsibilities
• Extract and analyze EHR data
• Ensure data accuracy and system efficiency
• Support EHR implementation and optimization
• Generate reports from EHR systems
• Collaborate with IT and clinical teams
Requirements
• Degree in Health Informatics, IT, or related field
• Familiarity with EHR/EMR systems
Skills
• Health informatics
• Data extraction and analysis
• System knowledge
