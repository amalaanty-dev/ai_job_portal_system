2. Clinical Data Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes clinical data from hospitals, labs, or clinical trials to support medical research and
patient care improvements.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze patient and clinical trial data
• Validate and clean clinical datasets
• Support research teams with data insights
• Generate reports on treatment outcomes
• Ensure compliance with clinical data standards
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Pharmacy, or Healthcare Analytics
• Understanding of clinical workflows and medical terminology
Skills
• Clinical data interpretation
• Knowledge of research methodologies
• Data validation techniques
