7. Public Health Data Analyst (Entry-Level)
Job Summary
Analyzes population health data to support public health programs and policies.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and analyze public health datasets
• Track disease trends and outbreaks
• Support health policy development
• Prepare reports for government/public health bodies
• Work with epidemiological data
Requirements
• Degree in Public Health, Statistics, or Epidemiology
• Basic knowledge of public health systems
Skills
• Statistical analysis
• Research skills
• Data interpretation
