12. Population Health Analyst
Job Summary
Analyzes population-level data to improve community health outcomes and reduce healthcare
costs.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze epidemiological and population health data
• Identify high-risk patient groups
• Support preventive care strategies
• Develop population health dashboards
• Collaborate with public health teams
Requirements
• Degree in Public Health, Epidemiology, or Statistics
• 2–5 years of experience in health analytics
Skills
• Statistical modeling
• Public health knowledge
• Data interpretation
