46. Insurance Claims Analyst
Job Summary
Evaluates healthcare insurance claims to ensure proper processing, detect discrepancies, and
reduce financial risks.
Key Responsibilities
• Review and analyze insurance claims data
• Identify claim denials and processing issues
• Detect fraudulent or suspicious claims
• Prepare claims analysis reports
• Collaborate with insurance and billing teams
Requirements
• Degree in Insurance, Finance, or Healthcare Analytics
• Experience in claims processing or insurance domain
Skills
• Claims analysis
• Fraud detection
• Data interpretation
