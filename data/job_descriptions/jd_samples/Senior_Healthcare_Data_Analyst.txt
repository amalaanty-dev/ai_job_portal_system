19. Senior Healthcare Data Analyst
Job Summary
Leads complex data analysis projects, delivering actionable insights to improve clinical
outcomes, operations, and financial performance.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform advanced data analysis on large healthcare datasets
• Design predictive models and analytical frameworks
• Lead end-to-end analytics projects
• Mentor junior analysts and review their work
• Communicate insights to senior stakeholders
Requirements
• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Data Science, Healthcare Analytics, or related field
• 5+ years of experience in healthcare data analysis
Skills
• Advanced SQL, Python/R
• Statistical modeling
• Leadership & mentoring
