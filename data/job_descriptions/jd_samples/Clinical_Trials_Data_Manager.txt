33. Clinical Trials Data Manager
Job Summary
Manages the lifecycle of clinical trial data, ensuring quality, integrity, and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and manage clinical trial databases
• Oversee data collection, entry, and validation processes
• Ensure data accuracy and audit readiness
• Coordinate with clinical teams and CROs
• Maintain documentation for regulatory submissions
Requirements
• Degree in Life Sciences, Clinical Research, or related field
• Experience in clinical data management
Skills
• CDMS tools (Medidata, Oracle Clinical)
• Data governance
• Project coordination
